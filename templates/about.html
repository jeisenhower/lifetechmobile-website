About Us

LifeTech Mobile began about a year ago with the idea of selling pocket printers. Since then it has evolved through the skillset of its founders to provide tech solutions to problems ranging from inventory tracking and movement to cinematography tools, to web development.

We are always looking to branch out and apply our skillset to new areas. Creative problem solving is our passion at LifeTech Mobile, and we strive to provide quality products and services for the betterment of the community and its people.

What We Do

At LifeTech Mobile, we provide technology solutions to both common and unique problems. We pride ourselves in our ability to learn rapidly and apply our knowledge to real-world problems.
